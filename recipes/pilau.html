Pilau Recipe

Description

Pilau is a Swahili community dish of East Africa. It's mainly prepared during holidays and various special occasions like marriage ceremonies. It's a mouthwatering meal and easy to cook.

Ingredients

  • 12Kg Meat (mutton, beef or chicken)
  • 12Kg Rice (basmati rice recommended)
  • 112tea-spoon Pilau masala
  • 12tea-spoon salt
  • 3 cloves of garlic (medium sized)
  • 2 red onions (medium size)
  • ginger (small piece)
  • 2 tomatoes (medium size)
  • Coriander (a small bunch)
  • 4 table-spoons of Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into medium size pieces
  2. Wash the meat with clean water and keep aside
  3. Wash the rice in a clean container and keep it aside
  4. Cut one onion into small pieces and keep it aside
  5. Peel the ginger and grind it
  6. Peel the cloves of garlic, crush them and keep aside
  7. Put the cooking oil in a dry cooking pot
  8. Put on the cooker and place your cooking pot on it
  9. Put the red onion pieces in the cooking pot before the cooking oil becomes hot
  10. Fry the onions with a medium heat until they turn golden yellow
  11. Add garlic onions and ginger fry for about a minute
  12. Add meat, stir until well mixed and cook for about 3 minutes
  13. Add pilau masala and mix well
  14. Add 2 and half cups (medium size) of water
  15. Allow to boil for 15 minutes (high heat)
  16. Add rice and cook in a medium heat until almost dry
    17. (remember to stir the rice 2-3 times before drying)
  17. When almost dry cover it with a lid and cook in a low heat for about 10 minutes
    18. While the rice is drying prepare kachumbari to serve with

Steps for preparing kachumbari

Using the remained ingredients
  1. Cut the red onion into small pieces
  2. Cut the tomatoes (small pieces) in the same plate with onions
  3. Cut the coriander in same plate with pieces of onions and tomatoes
  4. Add salt according to your taste and mix well
